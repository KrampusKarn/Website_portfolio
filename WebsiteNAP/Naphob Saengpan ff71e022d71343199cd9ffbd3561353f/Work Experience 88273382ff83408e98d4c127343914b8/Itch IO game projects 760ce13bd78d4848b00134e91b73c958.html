Brackeys Game Jam event

  • Aooa Nam Nin (อ่าวน้ำนิล: The Ebony Gulf)

Description

  • Dive into the deep, dark tide. Explore vast bodies of water where nature has no mercy, ride on your one-of-a-kind submarine, and push deeper to accomplish your deadly mission.

    Manage your resources wisely. You may not last long on your journey with your limited supplies, and you will not be given a second chance in the deep sea. Ledge yourself around the submarine or swim further to grab whatever you can and salvage materials dropped by previous ships to keep your submarine running.

    The company-supplied headlamp and submersible lights will help you illuminate the dark sea, but they will occasionally fail. You must repair the provided equipment before returning to the dock and throughout the mission. But most importantly, you must return to the surface while still breathing.
